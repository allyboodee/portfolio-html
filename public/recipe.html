Recipe for an Ally Salad

Ingredients

For the dressing:

Pulse in a food processor until smooth. (Or just use olive oil and a lemon wedge instead!)

  • 2 cups fresh cilantro, washed
  • 1/4 cup fresh squeezed lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • optional: add 1 avocado or 1/2 cup Greek yogurt

For the toppings:

Choose any five.

  • carrots
  • cucumbers
  • celery, sliced very thin
  • red cabbage, sliced thin: plain or mixed with a little lime and mayo
  • broiled and chilled cauliflower
  • blanched and chilled asparagus
  • steamed and chilled broccoli
  • yellow pepper
  • broiled and chilled zucchini
  • fuji apple
  • feta or goat cheese
  • capers
  • garbanzo beans
  • herbs: dill, italian parsley, and/or cilantro
  • artichoke hearts, canned or jarred

Assembly:

  • Make a base of at least two kinds of lettuce, freshly washed and chilled.*
  • Select at least two toppings that have been cooked.
  • Add your favorite protein and make it a whole meal.

*Try to avoid prewashed lettuce packages due to their high content of chlorine, increased liklihood of bacteria contaminants, and unfriendliness to farms and the planet.
